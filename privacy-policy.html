What Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal data:

  • Identity Data – name, job title, company name
  • Contact Data – email address, phone number, postal address
  • Technical Data – IP address, browser type/version, device information
  • Usage Data – website interaction, page views, referral sources
  • Communication Data – correspondence you send to us
  • Marketing Preferences – opt-in preferences for communications

How We Collect Your Data

  • Directly from you (e.g. contact forms, service enquiries, support requests)
  • Automatically via website analytics tools and cookies
  • From third parties (e.g. service partners, referral sources)

Legal Bases for Processing

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent – when you've given us permission (e.g. marketing)
  • Contract – to fulfil a contract or respond to service requests
  • Legal Obligation – when required by law
  • Legitimate Interests – for administrative, operational or marketing purposes (without overriding your rights)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide products or services to you
  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • To maintain and improve our website and services
  • To send you marketing communications (where consent is given)
  • To comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Website and hosting providers
  • Email and CRM service providers
  • Legal and professional advisers
  • Law enforcement or regulators (if required by law)

All partners are required to adhere to data protection laws.

International Transfers

If we transfer your data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law. Data is securely deleted when no longer needed.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: compliance@proactivetelecom.co.uk

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and collect anonymised analytics data. You can manage cookies through your browser or via our cookie banner. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

Data Security

We take steps to secure your data using:

  • Encryption (SSL/TLS)
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Secure hosting and firewall protection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, notified by email or site banner.
